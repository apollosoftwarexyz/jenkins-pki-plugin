Certificate Email Field

The field of the X.500 subject that contains the user's email address.

Your X.509 infrastructure may not be set up in this way (LDAP is likely a better fit for this property). In these cases, you should leave this field blank.


For example:

If your subject looked like "UID=jsmith,E=john@myorganization.com,CN=John Smith,OU=people,O=My Organization,C=GB"; you could supply E as the "Email Field" to get "john@myorganization.com" as the email address.

You would have to configure E as a custom attribute below as it is non-standard per RFC 2253.
