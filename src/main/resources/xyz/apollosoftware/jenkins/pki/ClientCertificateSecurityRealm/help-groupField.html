Certificate Group Field

The field of the X.500 subject that contains the user's group.

Your X.509 infrastructure may not be set up in this way - or there may not be a group component that is as granular as you need (LDAP is likely a better fit for this property in large organizations). In these cases, you should leave this field blank.


For example:

If your subject looked like "UID=jsmith,CN=John Smith,OU=people,O=My Organization,C=GB"; you could supply OU as the "Group Field" to get "people" as the group.
